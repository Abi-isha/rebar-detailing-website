Our Services

"RayMar" teams bring extensive expertise in reinforced concrete construction, having successfully delivered nearly 150+ projects in rebar estimation and detailing

High-Accuracy Quality Takeoffs

Reinforcement estimation for concrete structures entails determining the required steel and categorizing it by type, bar size, and bending specification, arranged both element-wise and segment-wise. We also include mesh, accessory, and concrete quantity details whenever they are part of the project scope. At RayMar Rebar, our experienced estimators utilize cutting-edge tools such as ASA, RebarCAD/CADSRC, Bluebeam, and Excel to generate accurate, detailed rebar estimates in diverse formats. This empowers our clients with comprehensive information, enabling them to bid with confidence and secure projects effectively.
Sample Work

Code-Compliant Detailing

We create reinforcement placement drawings based on the design plans, which contractors then use to fix the bars at the site. These drawings give clear instructions about the number, size, shape, and position of the rebar to be placed in the formwork before concreting. Our software also helps prepare Bar Bending Schedules in different customized formats, which can be fed into automated machines in fabrication shops. At Rebar Detailing, we use ASA or Rebar CAD/CADSRC to make shop drawings that follow local structural codes and construction standards. We follow a three-step review system to check quality and ensure smooth communication before delivering the final drawings to the client.

Fabrication-Ready Drawings

Our 3-Step Rebar Detailing Process

01

Structural Review & Estimation

We begin by thoroughly reviewing your structural drawings, specifications, and project requirements to produce accurate rebar quantity take-offs and define detailing needs for every structural element.

02

Detailed Rebar Modeling & Coordination

Using precise modeling tools, we prepare comprehensive rebar shop drawings, bar bending schedules (BBS), and placement plans, coordinating with structural and architectural inputs to ensure constructability.

03

Review, Refinement & Final Delivery

We support engineering reviews and revisions, refine drawings based on feedback, and issue fabrication-ready detailing packages optimized for smooth site execution.
